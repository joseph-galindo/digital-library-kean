								July 25[th] 
My Dear Miss Thompson ,
	Howie Lay and I have been "okayed" to be instructors in the Air Force  Technical Schools. 
	We'll be on this field a few more weeks, observing classes etc.  Then we go to Chanute Field, Illinois for a six week instructors course.
						Regards too everyone.
						Bob Salkin
