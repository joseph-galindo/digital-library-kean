                                                         FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
                                                                8 JANUARY 1946
                                                                              
The promotion of Sergeant of Clement T. Tetkowski of 173 Avon Avenue, Newark, New Jersey, was announced recently at Mason General Hospital, Brentwood, New York, where he has been stationed since November 1945 as an Educational and Vocational Counseller.

Sgt. Tetkowski served in Africa and Italy for eight months. He was graduated from the State Teachers College in Newark. His brother, Lt. John C. Tetkowski, is  Public Relations Officer at an Army Station in Kansas City, Kansas.
