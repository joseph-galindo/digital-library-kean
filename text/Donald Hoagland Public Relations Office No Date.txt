PUBLIC RELATIONS OFFICE
ARMY AIR FORCES NAVIGATION SCHOOL
San Marcos, Texas

SAN MARCOS Army Air Field, Texas  -  2[nd]. Lt. Donald P. Hoagland, 23, N.J. State Teachers College graduate in 1941, was a member of the largest class in the history of this AAF Central Flying Training Command field that recently received wings and commissions as aerial navigators in the Army Air Forces. Lt. Hoagland obtained a B.S. degree in Education. His parents, Mr. and Mrs. Hoagland, live at 18 Martin Road, Verona, N.J.
